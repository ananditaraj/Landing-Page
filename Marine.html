Founded in 2003 , Back Cove Yachts leads the industry in 34’ to 41’ Downeast-style motor yachts, with both shaft drive and outboard-powered options. More than 750 Back Cove boats can be found in harbours on both coasts of Australia, in New Zealand, and

from Norway to Greece in Europe. In North America, Back Cove boats have found their way to bodies of water from

all coastal regions to inland lakes and rivers.
